Regular questions & answers

Q: How do I place an order?

A: You can place an order by clicking on the "Products" tab and then select the products you want to buy. When you are done, click on the shopping bag in the top right corner and then click on "Checkout".

Q: How do I pay for my order?

A: You can pay for your order by using Swish, Klarna or credit card.

Q: How do I know if my order has been confirmed?

A: You will receive an email confirmation when your order has been confirmed.
